# Welcome to HabitQuest

HabitQuest is a habit tracking app that helps you build good habits and break bad ones. It provides a simple and intuitive interface for tracking your habits, setting goals, and monitoring your progress, 
applying gamification elements and RPG mechanics to make habit formation more engaging and rewarding. 
With HabitQuest, you can create custom habits, set reminders, and earn rewards as you achieve your goals. 
Whether you're looking to improve your health, productivity, or overall well-being, 
HabitQuest is here to support you on your journey to better habits.
